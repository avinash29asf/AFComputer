Who We Are

A F Computer is a trusted name in computer repair in Pune, specializing in expert support for desktops, laptops, printers, and all computer-related accessories. We provide comprehensive IT solutions including laptop repair, desktop repair, printer repair, and networking services.

Our services cover hardware repair, software installation, system upgrades, networking, and firewall configuration. We also deal in sales of new and refurbished laptops, desktops, printers, and accessories at competitive prices in Pune.

With a reputation for quick response, honest advice, and quality work, we proudly serve home users, small businesses, schools and organizations with complete IT solutions. We now operate from two convenient branches — Nigdi (Pimpri-Chinchwad) and Chikhali (Pune).

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and accessible IT support and computer services to homes, schools, and businesses across Pune. We aim to be the go-to destination for all computer-related needs — from repairs and upgrades to sales and AMC services — by consistently delivering quality work with transparency and integrity.

Our Vision

To build a technology-empowered community in Pune where individuals and businesses have access to dependable IT support, quality hardware, and digital services that help them thrive. We envision expanding to serve more neighborhoods while maintaining the personal touch and trust that our customers value.
