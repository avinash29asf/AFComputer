About A F Computer

A F Computer is a trusted name in computer hardware, software, and IT services, serving Pune and Pimpri-Chinchwad. We offer expert support for desktops, laptops, printers, and all computer-related accessories.

Our services include hardware repair, software installation, system upgrades, networking, and firewall configuration. We also deal in sales of new and refurbished laptops, desktops, printers, and accessories at competitive prices.

With a reputation for quick response, honest advice, and quality work, we proudly serve home users, small businesses, and organizations with complete IT solutions.

We now have two convenient locations — in Nigdi (Pimpri-Chinchwad) and Chikhali (Pune) — so we're always close to you. Visit your nearest branch or reach out online for reliable, affordable, and professional IT support.
