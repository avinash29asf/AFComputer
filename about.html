Who We Are

A F Computer is a trusted name in computer hardware, software, and IT services based in Pune. We specialize in expert support for desktops, laptops, printers, and all computer-related accessories.

Our services cover hardware repair, software installation, system upgrades, networking, and firewall configuration. We also deal in sales of new and refurbished laptops, desktops, printers, and accessories at competitive prices.

With a reputation for quick response, honest advice, and quality work, we proudly serve home users, small businesses, schools and organizations with complete IT solutions.

We now operate from two convenient branches — Nigdi (Pimpri-Chinchwad) and Chikhali (Pune) — making quality IT support accessible to more customers across the city.
