FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our website development services.

What types of websites do you develop?

We develop a wide range of websites including business websites, portfolio websites, corporate websites, e-commerce stores, landing pages, and custom web applications tailored to your specific requirements.

How long does website development take?

Development time depends on the complexity of the project. A simple website may take 1-2 weeks, while a full e-commerce or corporate website typically takes 3-6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

Do you provide responsive website design?

Yes, all our websites are built with responsive design principles. Your website will look and function perfectly on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices of all sizes.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we provide ongoing website maintenance services including content updates, security patches, performance monitoring, backups, and technical support to keep your website running smoothly.

Can you redesign an existing website?

Yes, we specialize in website redesign and modernization. Whether you need a visual refresh, improved functionality, or a complete rebuild, we can transform your existing website into a modern, high-performing platform.

Is the website SEO-friendly?

All our websites are built with SEO best practices including clean code, fast loading speeds, mobile optimization, proper heading structure, meta tags, and schema markup to help improve your search engine visibility.

What technologies do you use for web development?

We use modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, PHP, MySQL, and popular CMS platforms like WordPress. We select the best technology stack based on your project requirements.

Do you provide website speed optimization?

Yes, we offer website speed optimization services including image optimization, code minification, caching implementation, CDN setup, and database optimization to improve loading times and user experience.
