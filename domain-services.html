FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our domain services.

What are domain services?

Domain services include assistance with registering a domain name, renewing your existing domain, transferring a domain from another provider, configuring DNS settings, managing domain records, and securing your domain online.

How does the domain registration process work?

Simply choose a service plan that suits your needs and share your desired domain name with us. Our team will check availability, assist with the registration process, and configure the necessary DNS settings to get you started.

Can you help me transfer my existing domain?

Yes, we can assist with domain transfers. The process typically takes 5-7 days. Our team handles the entire transfer process and ensures your website and email services continue without interruption.

Do you provide business email setup?

Yes, our domain service plans include business email configuration. We help set up professional email addresses using your domain name so you can communicate with customers professionally.

Is WHOIS privacy available for all domains?

WHOIS privacy is available for most domain extensions but not all. Some TLDs have registry restrictions that prevent privacy protection. We will guide you on availability based on your chosen domain extension.

What is DNS configuration?

DNS (Domain Name System) configuration involves setting up the records that connect your domain name to your website hosting, email service, and other online services. We handle all technical DNS settings for you.
